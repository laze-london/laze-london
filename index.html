West London furniture restoration

A one-woman studio bringing tired, beloved furniture back to life with patient craft and considered fabrics. Each piece stripped to the frame, repaired with care, and finished to last.

Hand-finished pieces, made to be lived with

I'm Heather, a one-person upholstery studio based in West London. New Zealand born with Scottish heritage, and shaped by time spent living in Barcelona, I bring these influences into every piece I restore. Each chair, sofa, and frame is stripped back, properly repaired, and finished in fabrics made to last for years.
